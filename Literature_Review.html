Literature Review And Comparison of Personal Finance Management System

1. Design and Development of PFMS (2012)

TitleDesign and Development of a Personal Financial Management System
AuthorsBekaroo & Sunhaloo
Year2012
Advantages • 80% user retention rate over 3 months
• Established correlation between system responsiveness and engagement
• Set fundamental benchmarks for PFMS development
• Balanced automation with user interface design
Disadvantages • Older research may not reflect current technology capabilities
• Limited focus on modern payment methods
• May not address current security requirements
• Basic automation compared to current standards

TitleEnhancing Transaction Categorization in PFMS
AuthorsChen, Zhang, Wang & Liu
Year2023

• Requires significant training data
